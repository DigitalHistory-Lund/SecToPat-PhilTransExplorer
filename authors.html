Authors

Who wrote for the Royal Society, and when

Names are surface forms. Author counts group by the exact name string in the source metadata. Two spellings of the same person (e.g. Newton, Isaac and Sir Isaac Newton) are counted separately. 1,643 articles (19%) have no listed author.

The catalogue records 2679 distinct author names across 7,638 author-article links.
